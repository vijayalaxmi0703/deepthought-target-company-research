# Methodology
1. Approach
Selected Hyderabad as the target city and focused on specialty chemicals and biotech manufacturing companies aligned with DeepThought’s Federer ICP.
2. Sources Used
- Company websites
- LinkedIn
- MCA / Tofler
- Industry directories
- Google (targeted queries)
3. Filtering Process
- Removed CROs, testing labs, service companies
- Removed PE-owned and subsidiaries
- Excluded companies >500Cr revenue
- Ensured in-house manufacturing presence
4. Scoring Framework
Used 6 criteria:
C1 Manufacturer  
C2 India-based  
C3 Differentiation  
C4 Technical decision-maker  
C5 Sector tailwinds  
C6 Growth signals  
Each company scored with evidence.
5. AI Usage & Guardrails
- Used AI for structuring and initial discovery
- Cross-verified all data manually
- Removed uncertain or hallucinated info
- Validated manufacturer vs CRO carefully
6. Key Insight
Many Genome Valley companies are CROs — filtering them was critical to identifying true product manufacturers.
